Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Delivery Locations

We currently deliver across all major cities in Gujarat. Delivery to remote locations may take longer than usual.

Delivery Time

  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days.
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days (Available in select cities).

Shipping Charges

Free Shipping on orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹50 will be applied.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We only accept Cash on Delivery (COD) as a payment method. No online payments are required. The full payment is collected in cash at the time of delivery.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24-48 hours. You will receive a confirmation message once your order is shipped.

Missed or Delayed Deliveries

If you are unavailable at the time of delivery, our courier partner will attempt to deliver the package again. If the order is not received within three attempts, it will be returned to us.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions about shipping, feel free to contact us at stylenestt25@gmail.com or call us at (555) 123-4567.
