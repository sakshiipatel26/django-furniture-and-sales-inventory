Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Returns

We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 7 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition, unused, and with all tags intact.

Exchange Policy

If you receive a defective or damaged item, we will exchange it for you at no additional cost. Please ensure the product is in its original packaging.

Refund Policy

Since we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) as the only payment method, refunds will be processed at the time of pickup. Once our delivery team collects the returned product, the refund amount will be handed over to you immediately.

Conditions for Return

  • The item must be in its original, unused condition with all packaging intact.
  • Return requests must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products damaged due to misuse, improper handling, or regular wear and tear will not be eligible for a return.

How to Initiate a Return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support at stylenestt25@gmail.com or call us at (555) 123-4567. Our team will arrange a pickup at your convenience.

Need More Help?

If you have any further questions about our return and refund policy, feel free to reach out to us.
