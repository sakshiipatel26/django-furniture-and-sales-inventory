Brand Guidelines

Ensure consistency with our branding across all platforms.

Logo Usage

Our logo should always be used in its original form without modifications. Maintain clear spacing around the logo to ensure visibility.

Colors

Our brand colors define our identity. Use these colors consistently.

#0D47A1
#1976D2
#64B5F6

Typography

We use the Poppins font family for all text content. It should be used in a clean and readable manner.

Example: "StyleNest - Elevating Your Space"

Imagery

Our brand imagery should reflect high-quality, elegant, and modern furniture styles.

Tone of Voice

Our brand tone is professional, yet friendly and approachable. We aim to create a sense of warmth and trust in our communication.
