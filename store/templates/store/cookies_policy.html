Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how StyleNest uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better user experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing site performance.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for website functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us measure and improve website performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable personalized features like remembering login details.
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver targeted advertisements based on browsing activity.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience, such as:

  • Keeping you signed in.
  • Remembering your preferences.
  • Understanding how you interact with our site.

5. Managing Your Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services like Google Analytics to set cookies for tracking and analytics purposes.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie policy, please contact us at stylenestt25@gmail.com.
